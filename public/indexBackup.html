About NeoPapyrus

NeoPapyrus is an interactive digital library designed to rethink how we read and learn. Instead of static pages, our books are layered, visual, and adaptive — allowing readers to explore ideas at different levels of depth.

I am Prajwal Niraula, a postdoctoral researcher and the creator of NeoPapyrus. I have PhD from the Department of Earth, Atmospheric, and Planetary Sciences (EAPS) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).

From exoplanets to statistics and numerical methods, NeoPapyrus aims to make complex scientific ideas accessible without sacrificing rigor. Each book is designed as both a narrative and a tool for understanding.
